About the Bank

The National Bank of Ethiopia is the country's central bank, responsible for formulating and implementing monetary policy, supervising financial institutions, issuing currency, and supporting the sustainable growth of the national economy. We work to preserve price stability, foster a sound financial system, and promote economic development for the benefit of all Ethiopians.
